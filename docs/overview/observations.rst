Sky coverage
============

Figure 1, below, gives an impression of the sky coverage of DP2.
**Caveat:** a tract is shown on this map if it exists in the Butler sky map for DP2, and a tract outline being drawn does not mean that it will have a deep coadd image in every patch.

.. figure:: images/observations-1-dp2map-temp.png
    :name: dp2_map
    :alt: Skymap of DP2 images.

    Figure 1: A preliminary map of the DP2 regions. Open black squares mark the locations of tracts in the skymaps (regions pre-identified for the creation of deep coadded images). Blue quadrilaterals are 19-sided HEALPix, similar in size to the LSSTCam 9.6 square degree field of view, of a density map of the number of visits. This plot is generated by one of the :doc:`/tutorials/index`.
